# High Altitude Lab - Governance

## The Association

To support the goals of the High Altitude Lab project activities,
in 2026 we established a new association which operates under the name _“High Altitude Lab”_. 

The Association is registered on 16.03.2026 in the city of Nyon and operates in accordance with article 60 of the Civil Code of Switzerland.
